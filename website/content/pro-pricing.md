## Frequently asked

**Does Fenix-Pro need Fenix?** No — they're independent. But they compose well: authenticate and
download [instrument tokens](#/tokens) with Fenix, then stream them with Fenix-Pro using the same
headers and token shapes.

**Which brokers are included?** All [15 live-feed adapters](#/pro-overview) on Pro and Team; any
4 of your choice on Individual.

**Can I try before buying?** The Pro plan includes a free trial — see the project page.
